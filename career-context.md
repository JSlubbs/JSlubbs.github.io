## Narrative Strategy (established in prior session)

### The through-line question
Before selecting projects, I need to identify the consistent thing I bring to every role. The intro/bio should state this directly, and each case study proves it. Still TBD — options might include:
- "I make complex systems legible"
- "I build design infrastructure that scales teams"
- "I find the right problem before solving it"

### Portfolio structure: 3–4 case studies, not more

| Slot | What it shows | Likely candidate |
|------|--------------|-----------------|
| 1 | Strategic complexity + ambiguity | MCP Manager or Usercentrics |
| 2 | Scale / systems / leadership | Visco or design system work |
| 3 | Craft + execution depth | Conductor (already built) |
| 4 (optional) | Range / earlier work | Crunched, Cognex, or Predicata |

### Case study structure for Staff/Head level
Not "problem → solution → screens." Instead:
1. **The situation** — what was broken or missing at the org level
2. **Your role** — who you led, what you owned, what was ambiguous
3. **The decisions** — what tradeoffs were made and why
4. **The outcome** — business/team impact, not just shipped features
